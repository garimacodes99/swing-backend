💡 Risk Management Principles

1% Rule

Never risk more than 1-2% of your capital on a single trade. This ensures you can survive multiple consecutive losses without significant capital destruction.

Minimum R:R of 1:2

Every trade should offer at least 2x reward compared to risk. Asymmetric risk-reward allows profitability even with 40% win rate.

Position Sizing First

Calculate position size based on risk amount and stop-loss distance. Never decide quantity based on available capital alone.

Account for Costs

Include brokerage, STT, and taxes in breakeven calculations. Hidden costs significantly impact actual profitability.
