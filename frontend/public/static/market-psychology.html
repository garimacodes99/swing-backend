Market Psychology

Why good setups fail in hostile environments
Optimism → Excitement → Euphoria → Complacency → Anxiety → Fear → Capitulation → Despair → Hope

Fear Zone

  • High volatility
  • News-driven candles
  • False technical signals
  • Emotional liquidation

System response: Reject most trades

Greed Zone

  • Smooth trends
  • Controlled pullbacks
  • Broad participation
  • Follow-through moves

System response: Allow trend-aligned trades

Risk-ON Regime

  • Sector rotation works
  • Momentum respected
  • Higher win-rate environment

Risk-OFF Regime

  • Sharp reversals
  • False breakouts
  • Capital flight dominates

Psychological Traps

  • FOMO after large green candles
  • Revenge trading after stop-loss
  • Confusing volatility with opportunity
  • Overtrading during uncertainty

System Philosophy

  • The system aligns with psychology — it does not fight it
  • Activity reduces during fear
  • Capital is protected during uncertainty
