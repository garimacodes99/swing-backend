About Swing Intelligence

Building systematic trading discipline through rule-based analysis and capital protection

Our Mission

To empower traders with systematic, rule-based analysis tools that prioritize capital protection over activity, eliminate emotional decision-making, and promote disciplined swing trading through transparent, educational methodologies.

Core Principles

The pillars of our trading philosophy

1

Filter-First Methodology

We reject before we accept. Stocks must pass multiple strict filters (trend, volatility, breadth, conviction) before earning a swing score. Most opportunities are eliminated to protect capital from structural weaknesses.

Philosophy: It's better to miss 100 "opportunities" than to take 1 bad trade.
2

No Predictions, Only Analysis

We don't predict market direction. We analyze current conditions, measure structural quality, and quantify risk. Trading decisions are based on observable data, not forecasts or opinions.

Philosophy: Respond to what IS, not what you think WILL BE.
3

Risk-First Execution

Position sizing, stop-loss levels, and risk-reward ratios are calculated BEFORE entry, never after. Maximum loss per trade is defined upfront. Profit is what's left after risk management.

Philosophy: Know your maximum loss before you know your potential gain.
4

Systematic Over Discretionary

Rules replace emotions. Every decision is governed by pre-defined criteria. There's no "gut feeling" or "this time is different." Consistency comes from repeatable processes, not talent.

Philosophy: Discipline beats intuition. Systems beat emotions.
5

Education Over Signals

We teach methodology, not hot tips. Understanding WHY a trade qualifies (or fails) is more valuable than just receiving a list of stocks. Knowledge compounds. Signals expire.

Philosophy: Give a trader a signal, they profit for a day. Teach them to filter, they profit for a lifetime.
6

Transparency in Scoring

Every score is explainable. Every rejection has a reason. No black boxes. Traders deserve to understand how conclusions are reached so they can make informed decisions.

Philosophy: Trust through transparency. Clarity over mystery.

How The System Works

A systematic approach to swing trade analysis

📊 Market Intelligence

First, we assess the macro environment: Is the market in risk-ON or risk-OFF mode? Are breadth indicators supportive? Is volatility controlled? This layer determines how strict our filters will be.

🔍 Trend Filter

Price below SMA 200? Swing BUY rejected immediately. Directional bias matters. We trade WITH the trend, not against it. No counter-trend gambling.

📉 Volatility Filter

ATR too high = unstable. ATR too low = inactive. We need controlled movement. Excessive volatility destroys stop-losses. Insufficient volatility offers no reward.

🎯 Conviction Threshold

Multiple indicators must agree. If RSI says buy but price structure says no, trade rejected. Conviction = confluence. Isolated signals are noise.

🌐 Breadth Check

Is the sector moving? Is the broader market supportive? Isolated moves fail more often. Strong breadth = tailwind. Weak breadth = isolated risk.

✅ Score Generation

Only survivors get scored (0-100). The score measures QUALITY, not certainty. Score ≥ 70 = eligible for consideration. But even scored trades require personal risk assessment.

Why This Approach Works

🧠 Psychology-Aware Design

We built the system knowing traders are emotional. That's why rules are non-negotiable. The platform doesn't trust you to be rational in the heat of the moment—it enforces discipline for you.

  • Removes FOMO by filtering aggressively
  • Prevents revenge trading through mandatory rest periods
  • Eliminates confirmation bias with objective scoring
  • Protects against overtrading by limiting opportunities

📐 Mathematical Foundation

Every decision has a quantifiable basis. Risk-reward ratios, position sizing formulas, stop-loss calculations—all based on math, not mood. Numbers don't lie. Emotions do.

  • Position size = f(capital, risk %, entry, stop)
  • R:R must be ≥ 1:2 for consideration
  • Max risk per trade capped at 1-2% capital
  • Breakeven accounts for all transaction costs
