Explain My Score

How the swing score is constructed • Filter-first logic

What is the Swing Score?

Overall quality of a swing setup on a 0–100 scale.

0–40
Avoid
40–70
Watch
70–100
Eligible

Conviction Score

  • Measures agreement between signals
  • Defines minimum confidence threshold
  • Below conviction floor → trade rejected

Trend Score

  • Directional bias filter
  • Price below SMA 200 → swing BUY forbidden

Volatility Score

  • Based on ATR and price expansion
  • Too high = unstable
  • Too low = inactive

Market Breadth

  • Measures market participation
  • Strong breadth = tailwind
  • Weak breadth = isolated risk
If Trend ❌ → Reject
Else if Conviction ❌ → Reject
Else if Volatility ❌ → Reject
Else → Score & Rank
